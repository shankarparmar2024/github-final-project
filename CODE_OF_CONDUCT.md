# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation respectful and inclusive.

## Standards
- Be respectful
- No harassment

## Enforcement
Violations may result in removal.

## Scope
Applies to all project spaces.

## Attribution
Inspired by Contributor Covenant

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Explain that community leaders are responsible for clarifying standards of acceptable behavior and taking appropriate corrective action.
## Enforcement
Provide an email address or contact method where instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported.
## Enforcement Guidelines
Community leaders will follow these Community Impact Guidelines in determining the consequences for any action they deem in violation of this Code of Conduct (e.g., Correction, Warning, Temporary Ban, Permanent Ban).
